Order Policies & Information

Please review the details below for a smooth, delightful experience.

Delivery & Pick-Up

You can choose to pick up your custom basket at our location, or have it delivered straight to you or your recipient.

Delivery: Available within your area for an additional fee, depending on distance. Once you submit your order form, we'll confirm the delivery fee along with your personalized quote.

Pick-Up: We'll arrange a convenient time for you to collect your order.

Deposit

A 50% deposit is required at the time of order to secure your basket. The remaining balance is paid upon delivery or pick-up.

Alcohol Policy

If you'd like alcohol included in your basket, please contact us directly for guidance. You may provide the alcohol yourself, or we can pick it up for you — in which case payment for the alcohol is required at time of purchase.

Order Deadline

All holiday-season orders must be placed no later than December 15. For orders outside the holiday season, please reach out to check current availability.

Questions about delivery, pick-up, or deposits? Reach out via our contact page or on Instagram.
